Safety Tips and Known Issues

Welcome to our Safety Tips and Known Issues page. Your safety is our top priority. We have compiled this information to help you have a secure and enjoyable experience on our platform.

General Safety Tips

  • Verify Credentials: Always check if the breeder or seller is registered with recognized organizations.
  • Read Reviews: Look for reviews and ratings from other buyers to ensure credibility.
  • Price Check: Be cautious of prices that seem too good to be true. They could be a red flag.
  • Payment Methods: Use secure payment methods that offer protection and avoid direct bank transfers.
  • Personal Information: Do not share personal information until you are sure of the breeder's or seller's authenticity.

Steps to Verify Breeders and Sellers

  1. Registration: Confirm that the breeder is registered with organizations like The International Cat Association (TICA), Cat Fanciers Association (CFA), or Traditional Cat Association, Inc. (TCA).
  2. Visit: If possible, visit the breeder's cattery to see the living conditions of the cats.
  3. Health Records: Request health records and guarantees for the kitten.
  4. Ask Questions: Ask about the breeder's practices, including how they socialize and care for the kittens.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I verify if a cat breeder is reputable?

Verify if the breeder is registered with recognized breeding organizations, read reviews from previous buyers, and ask for health guarantees for the kittens.

What questions should I ask a cat breeder?

Ask about the health of the kittens, their vaccination records, the breeding practices, and if you can visit their cattery to see the living conditions of the cats.

What should I look for in a healthy kitten?

Look for clear eyes, clean ears, a shiny coat, and a playful demeanor. Also, check for any signs of illness or lethargy.

Why should I buy from a registered breeder?

Registered breeders follow ethical breeding practices, provide health guarantees, and offer lifetime support. This ensures you are getting a healthy and well-cared-for kitten.

Can I visit the breeder's cattery before buying?

Yes, reputable breeders will allow and even encourage visits to their cattery to see the living conditions and meet the kittens.
